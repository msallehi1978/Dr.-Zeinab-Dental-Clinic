Service 01

Dental Implants

Dental implants are a permanent and highly effective solution for replacing missing teeth, restoring both the function and aesthetic appearance of your smile. At Dr. Zeinab Dental Clinic, we utilise premium materials such as titanium posts that act as artificial tooth roots.

These posts are surgically placed into the jawbone, providing a stable foundation for custom-made crowns. Our advanced techniques ensure minimal discomfort and long-lasting results, helping you regain your confidence and dental health with a natural-looking finish that lasts for decades.

  • Premium titanium posts for maximum durability
  • Custom-made porcelain crowns matched to your natural teeth
  • Minimally invasive surgical technique with fast recovery
  • Results that can last a lifetime with proper care
  • Suitable for single, multiple, or full-arch replacement

Orthodontics
Braces & Aligners

We provide comprehensive orthodontic treatments designed to correct misaligned teeth and jaw irregularities for both children and adults. Using a variety of options including traditional metal braces, ceramic braces, and modern clear aligners like Invisalign, our specialists create personalised treatment plans.

Proper alignment not only improves your appearance but also enhances oral hygiene by making teeth easier to clean. Our goal is to achieve a functional bite and a perfectly straight smile that boosts your self-esteem and ensures long-term oral health and stability.

  • Traditional metal braces — reliable and cost-effective
  • Ceramic braces — discreet and aesthetically pleasing
  • Invisalign clear aligners — virtually invisible treatment
  • Personalised treatment plans for children and adults
  • Regular progress monitoring and retainer fitting

Cosmetic Dentistry
Hollywood Smile

Our cosmetic dentistry services focus on transforming your smile into its most radiant version. This includes popular treatments such as veneers, lumineers, and professional teeth whitening. We specialise in the "Hollywood Smile" procedure, which uses high-quality porcelain shells to mask imperfections like chips, stains, or gaps.

By combining artistry with dental science, we ensure that every cosmetic adjustment complements your facial features. Whether you need a simple whitening session or a full smile makeover, our clinic provides life-changing results that you will be proud to show.

  • Porcelain veneers & lumineers for a flawless finish
  • Professional teeth whitening — up to 8 shades brighter
  • Hollywood Smile full-arch transformation
  • Digital smile design — preview your results before treatment
  • Complements all facial features for a natural, radiant look
